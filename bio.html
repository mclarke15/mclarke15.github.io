About

Meredith Clarke was born in Concord, Massachusetts, the first town in the United States to ban the sale of plastic water bottles! Growing up Meredith loved messing around on the computer and fixing problems for those around her. She spent the summers mentoring young girls and maintains a passion for empowering young women in her community. In college she discovered her interest in computer science, designing websites and programs to fit others' needs. She is involved with the outdoors club and enjoys leading hikes for students who are new to the hiking world. Meredith is a strong leader and a passionate coder, but where does her heart truly lie? Cozying up to a good book next to her love, Colby the dog.
